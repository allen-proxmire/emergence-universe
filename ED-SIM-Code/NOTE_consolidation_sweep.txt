NOTE — Post-Law VIII Consolidation Sweep
=========================================

The consolidation sweep extends the canonical 4×9×11×5 parameter grid in three
directions: higher N (24, 28, 32), finer angle resolution (0.25° steps near the
tangent region), and finer drift resolution (0.005, 0.015, 0.025), producing
7,260 new (sweep + atlas) cells for a total of 9,240.  The unified manifold
remains single-sheeted at all seven N values: IC cells never appear outside
the boundary, DECAY remains strictly N=4-exclusive, co-transition rate is 100%
everywhere, and cross-layer agreement is 100% for every N≥8.  The complexity
flow continues: mean chi compresses from 31.6 (N=4) to 0.01 (N=32)—a
3,000-fold reduction—and the sub-mechanism count narrows from 9 (N=4) to a
minimum of 5 at N=28 (IC-direct, OPBC-single, OL-stalled, OPBC-glancing,
OPBC-winding).  No new sub-mechanisms appear at any high N; the only types lost
above N=4 are DE-tangent and DE-frozen, confirming Law VII.

Two minor wrinkles surface but do not compromise Law VIII.  First, N=24 shows a
faint OL-wandering echo (3.2%, vs. 22.7% at N=8) that inflates its mean chi
slightly above N=20; this trace vanishes by N=28.  Second, N=32 re-introduces
IC-bounced (19%) and CR-diagonal (4%) as geometric consequences of the larger
ring's circumradius-to-box ratio, ticking the sub-mechanism count from 5 back to
6.  Neither deviation introduces new topology, breaks single-sheetedness, or
creates a new resonance.  Finer angle resolution confirms that the DECAY filament
resolves smoothly at sub-degree precision with no hidden cusps, and finer drift
resolution confirms complete boundary rigidity at N≥8.  The temporal fold count
drops to zero at N=32, where chi is uniformly at the floor.  Law VIII—that the
five manifolds describe a single coherent geometric object undergoing a monotone
complexity-reduction flow in N—is confirmed by the consolidation sweep.
